Who We Are

A Wholistic Approach to Mental Health

Kelsuby Health and Wellness, LLC is a Psychiatric Nurse Practitioner practice dedicated to treating the whole person. We focus on mental disorders with a wholistic perspective, building agreements between patients, providers, medications, family, prayer, and therapy.

Our practice is built on trust, dignity, and compassion. We work with you, not just for you. Every care plan is individualized and rooted in evidence-based practice.

Patient-Centered

Your voice drives every care decision we make.

Family Involved

We welcome and encourage family participation.

Faith-Affirming

Prayer and spirituality are respected in our approach.

Evidence-Based

Clinical best practices guide every treatment plan.

Mental Health Services

Comprehensive Psychiatric Care

We see patients across a wide spectrum of mental health diagnoses and provide individualized, compassionate care at every step.

ADHD

Comprehensive evaluation and medication management for Attention Deficit Hyperactivity Disorder across all ages.

Anxiety Disorders

Treatment for generalized anxiety, panic, social anxiety, and related conditions using proven interventions.

Bipolar Disorder

Mood stabilization, medication management, and care coordination for bipolar spectrum disorders.

Depression

Assessment and ongoing treatment for major depressive disorder and related mood conditions.

Schizophrenia

Comprehensive psychiatric care for schizophrenia spectrum and other psychotic disorders.

OCD

Evidence-based treatment for obsessive-compulsive disorder and related spectrum conditions.

Personality Disorders

Structured support and medication management for various personality disorder presentations.

Medication Management

Regular psychiatric medication reviews, adjustments, and patient education for optimal outcomes.

Autism Spectrum

Supportive care, behavioral guidance, and medication management for individuals on the autism spectrum.

Substance Use

Treatment for substance use disorders including Suboxone and Vivitrol-assisted recovery programs.

Psychotherapy

Individual and family therapy services integrated with psychiatric care for comprehensive wellbeing.

Appointment Booking

Easy online appointment scheduling for new and returning patients. We make access simple.
